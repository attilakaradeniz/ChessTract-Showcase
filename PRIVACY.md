🇬🇧 Privacy Policy for ChessTract
1. Data Collection and Usage
ChessTract is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal identification data, chess analysis, or PGN files to any external servers. All chess games, databases, and analysis trees are stored strictly locally on your device.

2. Camera and Image Processing
The application requests camera permissions solely for the purpose of capturing chess notations from physical books (OCR). All image processing and text recognition are performed locally on your device using ML Kit. Captured images are never uploaded, stored remotely, or shared with third parties.

3. Third-Party Services
We use Google Firebase Crashlytics to monitor application stability. If the app crashes, completely anonymous crash reports (e.g., device model, operating system version, and error stack traces) are sent to help us identify and fix bugs. This data contains no personally identifiable information.

4. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
